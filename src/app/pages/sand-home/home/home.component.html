The objective of the UDPN sandbox is to demonstrate digital currency transfers and swaps through a single gateway, in a controlled environment. For further questions, please see our operation manual or white paper.

UDPN is a network comprised of 4 types of nodes. The UDPN does not interface with end users directly, but rather through IT systems connected to Business Nodes.

Business Nodes are the gateway to the UDPN.

Validator Nodes validate incoming message format and specific content.

Transaction Nodes connect to and executes transactions on digital currency systems.

In the future, an on-chain Transaction Audit & Reporting (TAR) Node will provide read-only access for auditors & regulators.
